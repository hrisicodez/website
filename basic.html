About Us

Our mission is to reduce food waste and hunger by connecting restaurants, supermarkets, and NGOs through smart technology.

How It Works

Donors list surplus food. NGOs get notified. Together, we make an impact.

Our AI-driven platform ensures efficient food redistribution to reduce waste and help communities in need.

Step 1: List Surplus Food

Restaurants and supermarkets input surplus food details (quantity, type, expiry date).

Step 2: AI Matching

Our AI system matches available food with nearby NGOs based on their needs and capacity.

Step 3: NGO Pickup

NGOs receive notifications and pick up food for distribution to those in need.
