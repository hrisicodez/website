How It Works

Our platform simplifies the process of redistributing surplus food, ensuring it reaches those in need efficiently and effectively.

Step 1: Register

Restaurants, supermarkets, and NGOs sign up on our platform and create their profiles.

Step 2: AI Matches

Our AI algorithm matches surplus food from suppliers with NGOs based on location, quantity, and type of food.

Step 3: Notify

Both the supplier and NGO are notified, and logistics are coordinated for smooth pickup and delivery.

Step 4: Redistribute

NGOs distribute the food to communities in need, reducing waste and fighting hunger.
